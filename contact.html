Frequently Asked Questions
How far in advance should I book?
Summer weekend departures sell out 2–3 weeks ahead. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks before your preferred date in peak season (June–September) and 1 week in shoulder season.
What's your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 7+ days before departure receive a full credit toward a future trip. Cancellations within 7 days are non-refundable but can be transferred to another guest at no charge.
Do you offer private group tours?
Yes. Groups of 6+ guests can request private departures on custom dates for any of our four Ontario routes. Contact us via WhatsApp for group pricing and availability.
Are tours accessible for limited mobility?
The Niagara Falls and Muskoka Lakes tours have the highest accessibility. Algonquin and Bruce Peninsula involve uneven terrain. Please contact us before booking and we'll advise on suitability.
What happens if a trip is cancelled due to weather?
Safety first — always. If we cancel due to severe weather or road conditions, you'll receive a full credit or refund within 48 hours. We notify you as early as possible, usually the evening before.
What is the minimum group size?
Minimum 4 guests per departure. If you're booking solo or as a pair, contact us and we'll happily match you with an existing group on your preferred date at no extra cost.
