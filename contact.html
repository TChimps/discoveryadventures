Frequently Asked Questions
How far in advance should I book?
Most summer weekend departures sell out 2–3 weeks in advance. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks ahead for peak season (June–September) and 1 week ahead in shoulder season.
What's your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 7+ days before departure receive a full credit toward a future trip. Cancellations within 7 days are non-refundable but can be transferred to another guest.
Do you offer private group tours?
Yes! Groups of 6+ guests can request private departures on custom dates. Contact us via WhatsApp or email for pricing and availability on private bookings.
Are trips accessible for people with limited mobility?
Some tours have limited accessibility due to terrain. Please contact us before booking and we'll advise on which trips suit your needs and what accommodations we can offer.
What happens if a trip is cancelled due to weather?
Safety is always first. If we cancel due to severe weather or road conditions, you'll receive a full credit or refund within 48 hours. We'll notify you as early as possible.
Is there a minimum group size?
Yes — a minimum of 4 guests is required for a trip to run. If you're booking solo or as a pair, we'll add you to an existing group. Contact us and we'll match you up.
